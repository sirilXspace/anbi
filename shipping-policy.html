Shipping Policy

Introduction:

At Anooca, We Are Committed To Delivering Your Orders Safely And On Time. Please Review Our Shipping Policy Below To Understand How We Process And Ship Your Purchases.

1. Order Processing

All Orders Are Processed Within 1-2 Business Days After Payment Confirmation. Orders Placed On Weekends Or Public Holidays Will Be Processed On The Next Working Day.

Once Your Order Is Shipped, You Will Receive A Shipment Confirmation With Tracking Details.

2. Shipping Coverage

We Currently Ship To Selected Locations As Supported By Our Logistics Partners. Delivery Availability May Vary Based On Your Location And Pin Code.

3. Delivery Timeline

Estimated Delivery Timelines Are As Follows:

  • Local / Domestic Orders: 3-7 Business Days
  • Remote Or Out-Of-Service Areas: May Take Additional Time

Delivery Timelines Are Estimates And May Vary Due To Factors Beyond Our Control, Including Weather Conditions, Courier Delays, Or Regulatory Requirements.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping Charges, If Applicable, Will Be Calculated And Displayed At Checkout Before Order Confirmation. Promotional Offers Or Minimum Order Value Benefits May Include Free Shipping.

5. Cash On Delivery (COD)

Cash On Delivery Is Available For Selected Locations And Orders, Subject To Verification. Anooca Reserves The Right To Disable COD For Certain Pin Codes Or High-Value Orders.

6. Delayed Or Failed Deliveries

In Case Of Delivery Delays, Our Support Team Will Assist In Coordinating With The Courier Partner. If Delivery Attempts Fail Due To Incorrect Address Details Or Customer Unavailability, Re-Shipping Charges May Apply.

7. Damaged Packages

If The Package Appears Tampered With Or Damaged At The Time Of Delivery, Please Refuse To Accept The Order And Notify Us Immediately. If Damage Is Discovered After Delivery, Contact Us Within 48 Hours With Clear Images Or Videos For Assistance.

8. International Shipping

International Shipping, If Available, Is Subject To Customs Clearance, Duties, And Taxes Of The Destination Country. These Charges Are The Responsibility Of The Customer And Are Non-Refundable.

9. Address Accuracy

Customers Are Responsible For Providing Accurate And Complete Shipping Details. Anooca Will Not Be Responsible For Delivery Failures Due To Incorrect Or Incomplete Address Information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Stuck On Something? We're Here To Help With All Your Questions And Answers In One Place

1. What Products Does Anooca Offer?

Anooca Offers A Curated Collection Of Premium Perfumes, Luxury Gift Hampers, Fragrance Sets, And Corporate Gifting Solutions. Our Products Are Designed For Personal Use, Special Occasions, And Meaningful Gifting Experiences.

2. Are Anooca Perfumes Authentic?

Yes. All Anooca Products Are 100% Authentic And Carefully Curated To Meet High Quality Standards. We Ensure Originality, Premium Packaging, And Secure Sourcing For Every Fragrance We Offer.

3. Do You Offer Cash On Delivery (COD)?

Yes, Cash On Delivery Is Available For Selected Locations And Orders. COD Availability Will Be Shown At Checkout Based On Your Delivery Address.

4. How Long Does Delivery Take?

Orders Are Usually Processed Within 1-2 Business Days. Delivery Typically Takes 3-7 Business Days, Depending On Your Location. Remote Areas May Require Additional Time.

5. How Can I Track My Order?

Once Your Order Is Shipped, You Will Receive Tracking Details Via Email Or SMS. You Can Use This Information To Track Your Delivery Through Our Courier Partner.

6. What Is Your Return Policy?

Returns Are Accepted Only For Damaged, Defective, Or Incorrect Items Reported Within 48 Hours Of Delivery. Due To Hygiene Reasons, Opened Or Used Perfumes And Gift Items Are Not Eligible For Return.

7. What Should I Do If I Receive A Damaged Product?

If Your Order Arrives Damaged Or Incorrect, Please Contact Us Within 48 Hours Of Delivery With Clear Images Or Videos. Our Support Team Will Assist You With A Replacement Or Refund As Per Our Policy.

8. Can I Cancel My Order After Placing It?

Orders Can Be Cancelled Only Before They Are Shipped. Once Dispatched, Cancellations Are Not Permitted.

9. Do You Offer Gift Wrapping Or Special Packaging?

Yes, Many Of Our Gift Hampers And Sets Come With Elegant Packaging. Gift-Ready Options Are Clearly Mentioned On Product Pages Where Available.

10. Do You Provide Corporate Gifting Services?

Yes. We Offer Customized Corporate Gifting Solutions Tailored To Your Brand And Occasion. For Bulk Orders Or Customization Requests, Please Contact Us Directly.

11. Are The Prices Inclusive Of Taxes?

Yes. All Prices Displayed On The Website Are Inclusive Of Applicable Taxes Unless Stated Otherwise.

12. Is My Payment Information Secure?

Absolutely. We Use Secure And Trusted Payment Gateways With Encryption To Ensure Your Payment Information Is Safe And Protected At All Times.

13. Do You Ship Internationally?

International Shipping May Be Available For Selected Locations. Customs Duties And Taxes, If Applicable, Will Be The Responsibility Of The Customer.

14. How Can I Contact Customer Support?

You Can Reach Us Through The Contact Us Page On Our Website. Our Support Team Will Get Back To You As Quickly As Possible.
